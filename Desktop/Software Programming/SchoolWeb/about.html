About us

Preparing curious learners for meaningful futures.

At Evergreen Academy, we partner with each family to create personalized learning pathways, develop leadership, and inspire a lifelong love of discovery.

Our mission

We cultivate a supportive environment where academic rigor, social responsibility, and emotional wellness are equally valued. Students leave Evergreen equipped to think critically, act kindly, and solve complex problems.

Our values

  • Academic integrity
  • Inclusive community
  • Creative expression
  • Global citizenship
